My Favourite Person

Didi

My elderly sister is a remarkable woman who has always been a source of strength, wisdom, and compassion in my life. With her gentle voice and calm demeanor, she carries a sense of grace that age has only deepened. Over the years, she has faced challenges with quiet resilience, never letting life’s hardships break her spirit. She often shares stories from the past—moments of joy, hardship, and growth—that offer lessons no book ever could. Her memory is a treasure chest of family history, and I’ve learned so much from her about our roots and the values we were raised with. Even in her later years, she remains active in her community, always ready to lend a hand or a listening ear. She’s the kind of person who remembers birthdays, gives thoughtful advice, and makes everyone feel important. Watching her age with such dignity has been both humbling and inspiring. She shows me every day that getting older doesn’t mean fading away—it means shining with a different kind of light. I am incredibly lucky to have her in my life, not just as a sister but as a role model, mentor, and lifelong friend.
